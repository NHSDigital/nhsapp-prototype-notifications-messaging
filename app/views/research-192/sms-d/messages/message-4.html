Getting started with your NHS App

Wherever you are in the app, you can select the Help icon to see a help page about that screen. Get started by trying out some of the features below.

Request and manage repeat prescriptions

Request repeat prescriptions and manage your ordered medicines, including which pharmacy you collect them from.

Book and manage your appointments

Check for available GP appointments, manage hospital appointments and referrals. It may also be possible to contact your GP surgery using an online form through the app.

View your GP health record

If your GP surgery has given you access, you can view your health record and test results.

More things you can do with the app

You can also use the app to:

  • use 111 online to get medical help
  • find health information about hundreds of conditions and treatments
  • read messages from your healthcare services
  • register your organ donation decision
  • choose if your data can be used for research and planning
  • manage health services for other people who you help to look after We regularly update the app with improvements and new features. Select the Feedback button from any point in the app to tell us what you think.
